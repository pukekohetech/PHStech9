How Workshop Ready works

Complete each mission: answer the required number of safety questions correctly to earn its badge.

Not yet: an incorrect answer gives a useful hint and replaces the question with a new version of the same skill. You must then show the skill correctly.

Workshop map: place safety locations from your real room, then show the map to your teacher for confirmation.

Certificate: records completion of this learning activity only. It does not authorise use of any machine. Individual training and teacher approval are still required.

Emergency information: room-specific details are entered in Teacher Settings. When details are not entered, the app tells students to follow their teacher and the school's current procedure.
